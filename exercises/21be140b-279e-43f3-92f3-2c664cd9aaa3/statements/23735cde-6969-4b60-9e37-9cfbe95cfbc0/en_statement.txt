<p>The action of updating records through the <code><em>UPDATE</em></code> statement can also be used with queries to perform more complex data modifications. Queries can be part of any of the elements of the <code><em>UPDATE</em></code> statement.</p><p><img alt="" src="https://ikastaroak .birt.eus/edu/argitalpen/backupa/20200331/1920k/es/DAMDAW/BD/BD06/es_DAMDAW_BD06_Contenidos/BD06_CONT_R32_UPDATE_SELECT_miniatura.png" /></p><h3>Example</h3><p>Department 50 is the one with the lowest average salary. We are going to increase it by increasing the salary of employees who are below the average of their department colleagues.
